When There Are High Risks in Equestrian Sports, What Should We Do About Insurance?

However, they also carry a significant level of risk. Horses are unpredictable animals, and despite the most rigorous training, accidents can and do happen. According to the American Medical Equestrian Association, approximately 20% of equestrians will suffer a serious injury at some point in their riding careers.
